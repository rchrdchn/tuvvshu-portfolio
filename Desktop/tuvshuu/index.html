DIVERSE

As a little girl, I lived in a Ger, which is a traditional Mongolian housing from the ancient nomads. Without realizing, I was inspired by the space, full of patterns painted on the wooden structure of the Ger that my grandpa had handcrafted by himself.

I believe that my arts & craftsmanship skills are inherited from both of my grandpas as well as my dad. I studied fine arts in high school and attended the Institute of Fine Arts in Mongolia before I emigrated to the U.S.

Last year, I made a courageous decision to leave my architectural profession.
